Privacy Policy

Last updated: 17 June 2026

1. Overview

Ohmatic is a local-first application. It runs on your own computer. Inference happens on your device, and the people behind Ohmatic do not run any server that receives your prompts or the circuits you generate. There is no account, and the application contains no analytics or telemetry components.

We do not collect, sell, or share your personal information.

2. What stays on your device

Everything you type and everything Ohmatic generates stays on your machine. Prompts, circuits, parts lists, and settings are processed locally on your device and are not sent to us, with the single exception of the optional parts feature in section 3.

3. The only third-party connection: parts and procurement

The single point where Ohmatic can reach outside your device is the optional parts and procurement feature, which runs after a design is verified:

  • Distributor links. When you open a buy-link for a component distributor (for example DigiKey, Newark, Jameco, or LCSC), your browser navigates to that distributor's own website. That distributor, and for affiliate links an affiliate network, can see that visit and may set their own cookies under their own policies. See our Affiliate Disclosure.
  • Optional product lookup. If you turn on the optional Jameco product-lookup connector, Ohmatic sends the search terms for a part (such as its description and value) to the affiliate network's product-search service to fetch matching products. This connector is off unless you configure it, and it sends only those part search terms, not your full circuit or any personal information.

Apart from this feature, the application makes no outbound network requests other than to services running locally on your own computer.

4. This website

This website is a set of static pages. It uses self-hosted fonts, sets no cookies, runs no analytics, and loads no third-party resources. As with any website, the provider that serves these pages (currently GitHub Pages) may keep standard server access logs, such as IP address and the pages requested, under its own policy.

5. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights over personal data under laws such as the EU and UK General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the California Consumer Privacy Act as amended (CCPA and CPRA), including rights to access, correct, or delete personal data. Because Ohmatic does not collect personal data, we normally hold nothing to act on. If you believe otherwise, contact us and we will respond. We do not sell or share personal information as those terms are defined under California law.

6. Children's privacy

Ohmatic is not directed to children under the age of 16, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above.
