Affiliate Disclosure

Last updated: 14 June 2026

In plain terms

Ohmatic takes part in affiliate and referral programs run by component distributors and their affiliate networks. If you buy parts after following certain links that Ohmatic shows, Ohmatic may earn a commission. This comes at no extra cost to you.

This disclosure is provided in line with the United States Federal Trade Commission guidance on endorsements and material connections (16 CFR Part 255).

Why these links exist

Ohmatic is a source-available project (Ohmatic Source-Available License). Any commission it earns helps fund its continued development.

How it works in the app

  • After a design passes verification, Ohmatic can build a parts list and offer links to distributors such as DigiKey, Newark, Jameco, and LCSC.
  • Some of these are plain search links that earn nothing. Others are affiliate links.
  • Where a link is an affiliate link, Ohmatic shows a short commission notice next to that link, so the connection is clear at the point where you might click it.

What this does not change

  • The price you pay. Affiliate commissions are paid by the distributor or network. Nothing is added to your order.
  • Which parts are recommended. The parts list comes from your verified circuit, not from any commission.

Programs we use

Where affiliate links are available, they run through the following distributor programs and their affiliate networks:

  • DigiKey, through the Impact network.
  • Newark and element14, through CJ Affiliate.
  • Jameco, through AvantLink.
  • LCSC, which has no affiliate program, so its links are always plain.

Ohmatic earns a commission only after it has been accepted into a given program and the link is configured as an affiliate link. Until then, the matching links are shown as plain links that earn nothing.

Your choice

You are never required to use these links. You can search for any part directly at any distributor, or buy it anywhere you prefer.
