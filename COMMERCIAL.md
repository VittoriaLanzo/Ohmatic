Ohmatic is source-available under the Ohmatic Source-Available License 1.1
(Ohmatic-SAL-1.1), adapted from the Functional Source License 1.1 and the
PolyForm Small Business License 1.0.0. The license lets you use Ohmatic and
derivative works free of charge for any Permitted Purpose -- as long as your use
is (1) not a Competing Use and (2) within the Community Tier.

## You need no commercial license (Community Tier)

You are in the Community Tier, free of charge, if you are:

- an individual using Ohmatic for personal, non-commercial purposes;
- using Ohmatic for non-commercial education or research; or
- an organization that, together with its affiliates, in the prior tax year had
  fewer than 50 employees and contractors and less than $1,000,000 in revenue,
  and has raised no more than $1,000,000 in external funding.

Competing Use is never permitted at any tier without a commercial license,
including for Community Tier users.

## You need a commercial license -- or our written permission -- if any of these is true

- **Competing use:** you want to make Ohmatic -- or a product or service with
  the same or substantially similar functionality -- available to others, offer
  it as a hosted or managed service, or embed or redistribute it commercially.
- **Training on Ohmatic (any model):** you want to use Ohmatic, its weights, or
  its outputs to train, fine-tune, distill, or otherwise create or improve any
  machine-learning model or AI system. This is prohibited by default. Emailing a
  request grants you nothing: you receive no permission unless and until we
  approve your request in a written email reply. No reply means denied.
- **Scale:** your organization exceeds any Community Tier threshold above
  (revenue, external funding, or headcount).

Commercial licenses are negotiated individually and can include the rights above
plus support, warranty, indemnity, and SLAs. Written permission for training on
Ohmatic is granted, conditioned, or denied case by case at our sole discretion.
